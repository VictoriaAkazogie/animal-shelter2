Where dogs be.

Here at The Good Pup, we believe in creating an environment where dogs can thrive in and just be themselves

Learn social and life skills through fun training + interactive play

Curated Activities

All dogs are different and special in their own little ways. We want to honour that by letting them learn and grow at their own pace.

  • Planned based on each dog's progress
  • Use of Clicker Training
  • Fun and effective

Supervised Play

Good socialisation is the result of good experiences. With our trained handlers and two play areas, your dog will gain just that!

  • Our handlers go through intensive training before being qualified to manage the dogs
  • To ensure safety and comfort of all dogs, play groups are split according to their temperament and energy level

Safe & Clean

You dog's well-being is of utmost importance!

  • Anti-bacterial flooring
  • Air purifier to circulate clean air
  • Chill music + Aromatherapy
  • Assessment session for all dogs
